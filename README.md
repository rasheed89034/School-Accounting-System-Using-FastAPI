A comprehensive, end-to-end School Accounting and Management System designed to digitize administrative workflows. Built with a high-performance **FastAPI** backend and a responsive **Jinja2/Bootstrap** frontend, this ERP centralizes academic data, financial records, and quick-search capabilities to reduce manual overhead.

## ✨ Key Features & Capabilities

*   📊 **Real-Time Dashboard**: Instant overview of total students, staff, collected fees, and school expenses. Now features a unified **Quick Search Shortcut** for immediate access to the search portal.
*   🔍 **Advanced Smart Search Portal**: A dedicated, dual-interface search engine to retrieve records instantly:
    *   **Student Search (via Roll No):** Fetches the complete student profile, enrollment/joining date, and a detailed month-by-month **Fee History** showing all Paid and Unpaid invoices.
    *   **Staff Search (via Employee ID):** Instantly retrieves teacher/staff profiles, highlighting their designation, contact details, joining date, and basic salary.
*   👨‍🎓 **Student & Class Management**: Easily register students, assign them to specific classes, and define standardized monthly tuition fees.
*   💰 **Fee & Invoice Tracking**: Generate monthly fee invoices, track payment statuses, and manage financial records effortlessly.
*   👨‍🏫 **Staff & Payroll Management**: Maintain records of teachers and administrative staff along with their compensation structures.
*   📉 **Expense Tracking**: Log and monitor daily school operations and expenses to maintain a healthy financial balance.
